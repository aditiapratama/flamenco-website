About flamenco

Currenlty flamenco support Blender rendering jobs (using BI and Cycles), but it is designed to support a variety of software and tasks, such as compositing, simulation or encoding.

Thanks to its modular structure and simple RESTful API, it can be easily integrated in your pipeline.
